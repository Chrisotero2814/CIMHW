How to Make Brownies

  1. Preheat
    2. 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan
  2. Mix
    3. 2. In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.
  3. Bake
    4. 3. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.
  4. Frosting
    5. 4. To Make Frosting: Combine 3 tablespoons softened butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm.
  5. Additional topping ideas
    6. Some other fun things you can add to spice up your brownie include crushed nuts, dried fruit, shredded coconut, caramel pieces and marshmallows.
